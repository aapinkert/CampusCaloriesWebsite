Buy Home Game Tickets:

All students who are enrolled in fall 2016 courses are elegible to buy Home Game Tickets. Incoming Freshmen and Transfer students who are signed up for an Orientation session; and new Graduate/Professional students who are signed up for Orientation may purchase partial season packages. Purchase is subject to availability.

Buy Away Game Tickets:

All upperclassman who are enrolled in fall 2016 courses are elegible to buy Away Game Tickets. Incoming Freshmen, Transfer students, and New Graduate/Professional students are not elegible. An 86/14 split will occur for each away game, with 86% of each student away game allotment going | to undergraduate students, and 14% going to graduate students. Tickets will be offered daily based on credit hour ranges as of the Fall 2015 semester. Purchase is subject to availability.

Opt-in for Postseason Tickets:

All students who are enrolled in fall 2016 courses are elegible to opt-in for Postseason Tickets. Incoming Freshmen and Transfer students who are signed up for an Orientation session; and new Graduate/Professional students who are signed up for Orientation are also elgible. Opting in does not guarentee that you will recieve a ticket for that game, it only adds you to the request list. If Alabama goes to a postseason game, tickets will be allocated by credit hour.
